
## _(c. –4250 to –3620 BC)_

**[[1.4.1 — Founding of Khunzar]] (c. –4125)**  
Mountain dwarves establish **Khunzar** as the first royal seat of the realm that will be known as the **Kingdom of Thandralas**.

**[[1.4.2 — Founding of Avonnar]] (c. –4080)**  
Elven and allied arcanists on the distant northeastern islands raise **Avonnar** as a dedicated city of spellcraft and planar study.

**[[1.4.3 — Shaping of the Jotunbrud Court]] (c. –4040)**  
Frost giants pledge fealty to a single Frost King; the cult of **Thrym** gathers strength in the northern ice-citadels.

**[[1.4.4 — Birth of Forged Peoples]] (c. –3980 to –3810)**  
Dragonborn, goliaths, and other crafted or trial-forged peoples arise through divine and elemental intervention.

**[[1.4.5 — Gruumsh’s Children]] (c. –3890)**  
Orcs and goblinoids are first shaped and unleashed by **Gruumsh**, scattering across wild highlands and borderlands.

**[[1.4.6 — First Awakening of Humanity]] (c. –3715)**  
Human tribes emerge across temperate plains, coasts, and river valleys, still scattered and semi-nomadic.
