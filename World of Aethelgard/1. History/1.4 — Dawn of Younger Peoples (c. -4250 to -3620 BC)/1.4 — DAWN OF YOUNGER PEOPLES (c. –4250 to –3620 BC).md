
## **[[1.4.5 — Birth of the Forged Peoples]] (c. –3984 to –3813)**

Dragonborn and goliaths arise through divine and elemental intervention; other rare lineages appear in scattered, isolated origins.

## **[[1.4.6 — Gruumsh’s Children]] (c. –3891)**

Orcs and goblinoids are first shaped and set loose across wild borderlands under **[[Gruumsh, the One-Eye|Gruumsh]]**.

## **[[1.4.7 — First Awakening of Humanity]] (c. –3717)**

Human tribes appear across temperate plains, river valleys, and coasts—small, scattered, and largely semi-nomadic.
