## **[[1.4.1 — Founding of Khunzar]] (c. –4127)**

Dwarven royalty seat **[[Khunzar, the First King's Throne]]**, establishing the first enduring crown-hold of the realm later known as the Kingdom of Thandralas.

## **[[1.4.2 — Founding of Avonnar]] (c. –4083)**

On the northeastern inland isles, arcanists raise **[[Avonnar]]** as a permanent city of magical study and governance.

## **[[1.4.3 — Shaping of the Jotunbrud Court]] (c. –4041)**

Frost giants bind their court to a single throne; the cult of **[[Thrym, Lord of the Frost Giants|Thrym]]** becomes dominant in Jotunbrud.

## **[[1.4.4 — Norguhal Raised]] (c. –3992)**

The frozen citadel **Norguhal** is established as the central seat of frost-giant power in Jotunbrud.

## **[[1.4.5 — Birth of the Forged Peoples]] (c. –3984 to –3813)**

Dragonborn and goliaths arise through divine and elemental intervention; other rare lineages appear in scattered, isolated origins.

## **[[1.4.6 — Gruumsh’s Children]] (c. –3891)**

Orcs and goblinoids are first shaped and set loose across wild borderlands under **[[Gruumsh, the One-Eye|Gruumsh]]**.

## **[[1.4.7 — First Awakening of Humanity]] (c. –3717)**

Human tribes appear across temperate plains, river valleys, and coasts—small, scattered, and largely semi-nomadic.
