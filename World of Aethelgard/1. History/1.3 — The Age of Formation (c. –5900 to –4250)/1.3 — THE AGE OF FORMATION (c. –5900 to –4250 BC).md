
## **[[1.3.1 — Formation of the Emerald Enclave (c. –5891)]]**

The western great forest druid councils formalize under the [[Emerald Enclave]]; the first [[Heartkeeper of the Verdant Heart|Heartkeeper]] is elected; the council names the forest the [[Verdant Heart|Verdant Heart]]; [[Kymarine, the Great Forest City|Kymarine]] is founded as its capital (c. –5864).

## **[[1.3.2 — Formation of the Tidecallers Circle (c. –5807)]]**

The western sea-isles druid councils formalize under the [[Tidecallers Circle]]; the first [[Tidekeeper of the Tideshae Isles|Tidekeeper]] is elected; they name their home isles the [[Tideshae Isles]]; [[Aqua-El, the Coral Haven|Aqua-El]] is founded as its capital (c. –5794).

## **[[1.3.3 — Formation of the Kingdom of Thandralas (c. –5740)]]**

Dwarven clans of [[Durak-Throndhal, the Gods’ Anvil|Durak-Throndhal]] unify under the first [[Forge-King of Thandralas|Forge-King]]; the capital [[Khunzar, the Hammerhall|Khunzar]] is created within the volcano [[Durak-Tharum, the Gods’ Forge|Durak-Tharum]] and established as the first royal seat of the [[Kingdom of Thandralas]].

## **[[1.3.4 — Formation of the Realm of Jotunbrud (c. –5615)]]**

The northern ice-realm frost giants consolidate under the first [[Frost-King of Jotunbrud|Frost-King]], the mightiest of [[Thrym, Lord of the Frost Giants|Thrym’s]] children; within the same year [[Norguhal, the Glacial Fortress|Norguhal]] is raised as the seat of [[Frost-King Hyrm|Frost-King Hyrm’s]] dominion, and the land is named the [[Realm of Jotunbrud]].

## **[[1.3.5 — Formation of the Mystran Accord (c. –5420)]]**

The elven arcanists of [[Eyllisett, the Isles of Lore]] create the [[Avonnar, the Arcane Citadel|Arcane Citadel Avonnar]] and found the [[Eight Schools of Magic]]. The mages formalize as the [[Mystran Accord]] (c. –5415), establishing the [[Magistratum]] and the [[Council of Avonnar]] for governance and leadership.
