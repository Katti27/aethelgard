## _(c. –3020 to –1710 BC)_

**1.6.1 — Verdant Court of Eluthariel (c. –2975)**  
Western elven circles and high houses formalize the **Verdant Court** to mediate disputes and guide forest realms.

**1.6.2 — Thandralan Forge-Kings (c. –2890)**  
Dwarf High Kings in **Khunzar** consolidate rule over central mountain clans, codifying royal succession and rune-forge rights.

**1.6.3 — Isles of Lore Scholaria (c. –2815)**  
Avonnar’s arcanists organize the great colleges of magic that will evolve into the later **Magistratum**.

**1.6.4 — Opening of the Mystran Accord (c. –2680)**  
The elven Accord cautiously opens to a handful of non-elven apprentices and observers, while still being elven-led.

**1.6.5 — Early Human Heartlands (c. –2570 to –2120)**  
Human tribes define several main regions: western woodland fringes, central grasslands, northern riverlands, and southern coastal basins.

**1.6.6 — Varthash and the Western Courts (c. –2360 to –2180)**  
The bronze guardian of **Waveshadow Isle** serves as a neutral arbiter and protector between coastal mortals, elves, and dwarves.

**1.6.7 — First Fortified Human Towns (c. –2075)**  
Palisaded villages and hillforts appear along major rivers and coasts, marking the first stable human settlements.

**1.6.8 — Great Human Migrations (c. –1963 to –1648)**  
Centuries of climate shifts, monster pressures, and opportunity drive waves of human migration into new valleys, plains, and coasts, setting the stage for later realms.
