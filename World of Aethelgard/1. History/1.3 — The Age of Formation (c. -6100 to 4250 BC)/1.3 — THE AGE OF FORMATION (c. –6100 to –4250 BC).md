## **[[1.3.1 — Verdant Circles Coalesce]] (c. –5890)**  
Elven groves in the west begin to weave loose alliances between druidic circles and mage-singers.

## **[[1.3.2 — First Deep Delves of Durak-Throndhal]] (c. –5740)**  
Dwarven clans unite to carve the first great underhalls and name the central massif **Durak-Throndhal, the Gods’ Anvil**.

## **[[1.3.3 — Frost Kings of the North]] (c. –5615)**  
Frost giants consolidate high northern realms, establishing the first court that will lead to the **Realm of Jotunbrud**.

## **[[1.3.4 — High Elven Weave-Accord]] (c. –5480)**  
Select elven archmages form an early **Mystran Accord** to regulate high magic under Mystra’s guidance (elves only at this stage).

## **[[1.3.5 — Founding of Kymarine]] (c. –4330)**  
Western elves raise **Kymarine** as the first permanent high city of the great forest.

## **[[1.3.6 — Founding of Aqua-El]] (c. –4285)**  
Sea-linked elven clans in the western archipelagos found **Aqua-El** as their island capital and seaborne sister to Kymarine.
