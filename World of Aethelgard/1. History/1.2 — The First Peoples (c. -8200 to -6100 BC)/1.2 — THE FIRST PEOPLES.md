
## _(c. –8200 to –6100 BC)_

**[[1.2.1 — Awakening of the Elves]] (c. –8170)**  
Elves awaken in multiple regions of Aethelgard: the western great forest, the western sea-isles, and the far northeastern isles.

**[[1.2.2 — Awakening of the Dwarves]] (c. –8035)**  
Dwarves rise within the central mountain roots that will become **Durak-Throndhal, the Gods’ Anvil**.

**[[1.2.3 — Awakening of the Giants]] (c. –7920)**  
Giants emerge in the far northern ice and high peaks that will become **Jotunbrud** and other giant-haunted ranges.

**[[1.2.4 — Bronze Guardian of the Western Seas]] (c. –7810)**  
A mighty bronze dragon later known as **Varthash** claims **Waveshadow Isle** between the western forest and offshore isles, vowing to guard coasts and sailors.

**[[1.2.5 — Elder Wanderings]] (–7800 to –6400)**  
Elven circles roam the western woods, dwarven clans delve shallow holds, and giant hosts test one another in feud and contest.
