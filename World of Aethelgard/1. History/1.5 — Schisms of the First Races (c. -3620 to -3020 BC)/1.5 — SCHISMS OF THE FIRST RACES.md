## _(c. –3620 to –3020 BC)_

**[[1.5.1 — Drow Schism Begins]] (c. –3595)**  
A bitter arcane and religious conflict fractures elven society; exiled elves flee into the deep caverns.

**[[1.5.2 — Rise of the Drow Realms]] (c. –3470 to –3310)**  
Exile communities in the depths transform into the first **drow** cities, bound to **Lolth, the Dark Mother**.

**[[1.5.3 — Enslavement of the Deep Clans]] (c. –3410)**  
Illithids seize isolated dwarf delves in the deep underways; the ancestors of the **duergar** are taken as thralls.

**[[1.5.4 — Duergar Transformed]] (c. –3325 to –3170)**  
Illithid torment and experimentation twist captive dwarves into the grey-skinned duergar, who begin to serve elder brains.

**[[1.5.5 — Underdark Web Expands]] (c. –3250 to –3050)**  
Drow, duergar, and illithids carve and claim a growing network of deep passages beneath mountains and lowlands.
